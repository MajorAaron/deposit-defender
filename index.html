Why now

California now requires landlords to do this. Why aren’t you?

As of July 1, 2025, California AB 2801 obligates landlords to take high-resolution, date-stamped photographs before move-in and after move-out. Other states are following. DepositDefender brings the same documentation standard to your side of the lease.

How it works

  1. 01

    Walk the apartment.

    Phone in hand on move-in day. Snap every scuff, stain, gap, and crack — ten minutes is plenty.

  2. 02

    Upload to DepositDefender.

    We score your Deposit Defense Score 0–100, estimate dollars at risk, and generate a legal-language log entry your landlord can’t pretend they didn’t see.

  3. 03

    Send it before you sign.

    Email the documented walkthrough to your landlord on day one. When move-out comes, you’ve already won the dispute.
